About Us

This website sells a program that enables users to take tutoring lessons in French regardless of there level of knowledge. It is created to help people achieve their goal by allowing flexible hours, online courses and one on one online sessions. The website sells the program that the users download on their computer to use. The program has activities, exercises and short videos available for each user dependant on their needs and age. It will allow them to book and manage online tutoring sessions. It is easy to navigate and use. It is perfect for people that work or anyone with a busy schedule because you learn from your own home, no need to attend classes. Therefore, you can fit classes in your schedule without making any compensations.
