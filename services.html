Our Services

  • French Begginer Level 1 Program

    Level one is an introductory course. The program covers three main sections. First one is basic daily vocabulary. Second section is an introduction to conjugation. In this level there will be, passé compose, future simple and present. The third section is pronunciation, in this level the focus is on correct pronunciation and perfecting the French accent. This level has over 30 exercises. Fifteen, 20-minute videos and 15 quizzes to be done at the end of each video.

    After completing this level, students will be able to conjugate the mentioned forms of verbs. Students will also have basic knowledge of correct pronunciations.

    $ 50

  • French Begginer Level 2 Program

    Level two covers grammar and writing. The program covers two main sections in writing. The first is passé compose. The second is Future simple and Future proche.

    In this level there are twenty passages, with each passage you have to complete an exercise and a dictation.

    This level also has ten short stories. Five of them are in passé compose. The other five are in Future. There is are questions that go with the short stories as well as an online discussion board.

    After completing this level, students will be able to write short passages in passé compose and future form. Students will also be able to have basic vocabulary and written skills perfected.

    $ 70

  • French Intermidiate Level 3 Program

    Level three focuses on conversational skills. The program has audio exercise as well as video tutorial. In this level most of the exercises are audio. There are scenarios at workplace and school of conversational situations. This level will help you ask questions as well as answer. This level will further on work on the pronunciation and flow in speech.

    After completing this level, students will be able to have daily conversations, ask and answer common questions.

    $ 80
